Welcome to Burundi!

Burundi is a landlocked country located in east-central Africa. It’s one of the smallest African nations at 27,834 square kilometers (10,747 sq mi). This makes it smaller than Equatorial Guinea but larger than Rwanda. Burundi borders 3 other countries including the Democratic Republic of Congo to the west, Tanzania to the east, and Rwanda to the north. The capital city of Burundi is Gitega. But Bujumbura is the economic capital and the largest city.

There are approximately 11.6 million people who live in Burundi. It’s also one of the ten poorest countries in the world with a history of civil war, corruption, and HIV/AIDS transmission.
