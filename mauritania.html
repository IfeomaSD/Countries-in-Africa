Welcome to Mauritania!

Mauritania is a country located in Northwest Africa. It borders Western Sahara to the northwest, Algeria to the northeast, Mali to the east and southeast, and Senegal to the southwest. Its coastline with the Atlantic Ocean is approximately 700 kilometers (435 miles) in length. The country is known for its mining resources, history of slavery, and desert-filled landscape.

The population of Mauritania is approximately 4.4 million. Nouakchott is the most populous and is the capital city of Mauritania.
