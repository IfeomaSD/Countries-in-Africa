Welcome to Angola!

Angola is an African country located below the equator. It’s situated along the South Atlantic Ocean in the southern region of Africa. Angola borders the Congo and the Democratic Republic of Congo to the north. To the east and south, it’s also next to Zambia and Namibia.

The capital city of Angola is Luanda which is known for being the most expensive city in the world. Angola also contains the small exclave of Cabinda off the mainland. This region is known for its oil production and reserves
