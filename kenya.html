Welcome to Kenya!

Kenya is an African country located on the eastern coastline of the Indian Ocean. It lies on the equator between 5°N and 5°S latitudes. Kenya borders Tanzania to the south, Uganda to the west, South Sudan to the northwest, Ethiopia to the north, and Somalia to the northeast.

Nairobi is the capital and largest city of Kenya. Other major cities are Mombasa, Nakuru, and Eldoret. Overall, the country is known for its picturesque landscapes, savannas, wildebeest migration, Lakelands, and Great Rift Valley.
