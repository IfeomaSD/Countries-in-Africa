Welcome to Gabon!

Gabon is located in Central Africa, along the Atlantic Ocean at the Equator. It borders just 3 countries. First, Cameroon and Equatorial Guinea are situated to the north. Then, the Republic of the Congo wraps around Gabon from the south to the east.

Libreville is the capital and largest city just on the equator along the west coast. Its other major cities are Mandji, Masuku, and Oyem.
