Welcome to Chad!

Chad is a landlocked country that borders 6 other countries in Africa. Libya is located to the north, Sudan to the east, the Central African Republic to the south, Cameroon and Nigeria to the southwest, and Niger to the west. N’djamena is Chad’s capital and largest city, situated near its western border with Cameroon. Approximately 16 million people live in Chad.

Chad is known for its large swaths of desert that cover most of the northern region. Its primary economic activities are mining (like gold and uranium), oil production, and agriculture.
